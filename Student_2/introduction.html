My Food Journey Story

Hello! My name is Hasif and I am 14 years old. My journey into food blogging began with a deep passion for tasting different cuisines and learning how food connects us across cultures, borders, and generations.

Growing up in Malaysia during this historic year as ASEAN Chair 2025, I've witnessed firsthand how our region's incredible diversity in food reflects our shared values of inclusivity and sustainability. Every dish tells a story, every ingredient carries history, and every meal is an opportunity to build bridges between communities.

Through this blog, I aim to share healthy food discoveries, authentic recipes, and special places where you can explore cultural meals that support better well-being around the world. My mission aligns perfectly with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, particularly SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

As we celebrate Malaysia's leadership in ASEAN 2025, I'm excited to showcase how traditional Southeast Asian cuisines can lead the way in sustainable, inclusive, and healthy eating practices for our global community.

My Core Values

These principles guide my approach to food blogging and cultural exploration

Cultural Respect

Every cuisine has deep cultural significance. I approach food traditions with respect, seeking to understand and share their stories authentically.

Health & Wellness

Food should nourish both body and soul. I focus on traditional dishes that promote health while celebrating the joy of eating together.

Sustainability

Supporting local farmers, seasonal eating, and traditional preservation methods that protect our environment for future generations.

Community Building

Food has the power to bring people together. I believe in sharing knowledge and creating connections across cultures through culinary experiences.
