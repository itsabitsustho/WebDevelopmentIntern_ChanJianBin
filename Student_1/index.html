Project: Solaria is an educational initiative committed to advancing SDG 4 – Quality Education by providing engaging, accessible, and curriculum-aligned learning about space, the solar system, and space exploration. Tailored for primary and lower secondary students, the project sparks curiosity and builds scientific literacy through interactive, hands-on experiences. By combining creative teaching tools with real-world astronomical knowledge, Project: Solaria equips young learners with a strong foundation in understanding our universe—cultivating critical thinkers and inspiring the next generation of innovators in science and space technology.
