Principal

Soo Seng Poh Principal of Chung Ling High School

Mr. Soo became the principal of Chung Ling High School since 2017 which is the 100th anniversary celebration year. Since then, he manages the school systematicly and maintain our school image. He teaches Science using various interesting ways and treats his students well.

He usually teaches moral values during the assembly to make sure we are diciplined and obedient. He is the pinacle of our school which Chung Ling students respect and fear.
