Subjects

Languages








Mandarin 华文

Having grown to an unprecedented degree throughout the 21st Century, Mandarin’s significance cannot be understated. In view of the growing relevance of Mandarin, students in Chung Ling High School have continued to take strides in improving their proficiency in Mandarin.

Relative Weight: 7




Malay 马来文

Bahasa Melayu is the national language of Malaysia and as such in emphasised by the government in the pursuit of a diverse yet inclusive country.

Relative Weight: 6




English 英文

English is the lingua franca of the world and by far the most influential of any language in its outreach. Students in Chung Ling High School have grasped this fact and many students yearn to learn English and many of its nuances, be it through dazzling fictional worlds or through the spoken word.

Relative Weight: 5



Other Subjects










Science 科学

Science is the intellectual and practical activity encompassing the systematic study of the structure and behaviour of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment. Most of the Chung Ling students are fond of science and constantly produce good results in this subject.

Relative Weight: 5



Mathematics 数学

Since antiquity,Mathematics affords us the ability to analyse the world in a logical and intuitive manner.It forms the building blocks of science and it is fundamental to human nature in many respects.

Relative Weight: 5




Geography 地理

Geography is the study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere, and of human activity as it affects and is affected by these, including the distribution of populations and resources and political and economic activities.

Relative Weight: 3




History 历史

It is often said that those who don't learn from history are doomed it repeat in. Since time immemorial, there have been a rich tradition in the chronicling of events of the past.History is there to ensure that one learns about how the past has shaped the present and is a reminder that we must heed the warning of history.

Relative Weight: 3
