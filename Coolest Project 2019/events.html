Events

24 October 2019

Graduation Day

The Form 5 & Upper 6 students have finally graduated from this school. They will always cherish the time and memories they spent together in this school in which they cried, fought, learn and have fun. This was the day they parted ways to construct their own futures while hoping they would all meet again one day at the place they first met, Chung Ling High School.

24 August 2019

Penang Reading Programme 2019

The Penang Reading Programme is a state event to encourage the habit of reading to students throughout our state. Students bring their own books to school and read it for 15 minutes. The whole school was silent for that period of time, only the sound of pages flipping and birds chirping could be heard. All in all, it was a whale of a time.


Photos
23 August 2019

Thailand Visit 2019

The BASN (Bangkok-ASEAN Student News Center), a project for representatives of 7 students from 9 schools are travelling to produce creative documentary television programmes. Those documentaries act as a medium for communication, exchange of knowledge such as our culture and living habits. This year, they went to Singapore and Malaysia to conduct their project and Chung Ling High School is one of their places of interest.

Photos
31 July 2019

The retirement of Mr Chuah

Mr Chuah ended his job as a teacher and Form 6 senior assistant. He provided service for our school for more than 10 years in his life. He was a commited and dedicated teacher. He watched the hands of his students wave him goodbye as he left with heavy steps. That day was probably his most happy and sad day of his life.

24 July 2019

25th Tri-Chung Ling Sports day

The Tri-Chung Ling Sports day is organised by three schools, Chung Ling Butterworth, Chung Ling Private, Chung Ling High School to sharpen student's sport skills. This event also improved the relationship of students and teachers between these 3 schools. Students from different schools come together and compete with each other in various activities like 100 meter run, shot put and tug of war.

Photos
23 July 2019

Science Carnival

The 5th Chung Ling Annual Science Carnival was held on the 23rd of June 2019.Many students who took part in the science carnival and put in a valiant effort into their individual booths. The experiments encompassed anything from the dissection of a hamster to a Van de Graf Generator.The many primary school students who visited our school were inquisitive about the wonderful experiment and learnt knowledge beyond textbooks.

9-13 July 2019

IMUNC

IMUNC The International Model United Nations Camp 2019 was held in Chung Ling High School.It brought together students from all walks of lives to discuss, debate and collaborate on issues of significance to generation past and present. It also presented an opportunity for participants to experience the beauty of Penang first hand besides making new friends.

Photos

4 July 2019

Swimming Championship

The Swimming Championship is held annualy at Permata Sports Complex where students of our school compete with each other in swimming. This activity was aimed to lead student to a healthier lifestyle. The participants did their best and the winners recieved gifts to encourage them for their accomplishment
