21世纪竞争 岂敢后他人


“In the 21st Century, students have experienced a quality of life that was not even conceivable in the 20 years ago. With the advent of smartphones, social media, video sharing websites and much more, it has been insinuated that students are pretty well off. While that notion continues to ring true for the most part, educators have continued to express their belief that more should be done to improve the lives of students for unpredictable challenges in the future.“

“As humans move towards the 4th Industrial Revolution which is characterised by the unprecedented use of robots in all aspects of life, it had become increasingly difficult to make ends meet. It can thus be inferred that humans must compete among themselves and robots to sustain themselves in the near future.”

“Thus, it is imperative that our education system incorporates the needs of students by inculcating learning,creativity and cooperation in students. This is the principal reason that Chung Ling High School has always placed greater emphasis on co curricular activities than most schools as such activities broaden the horizon of students while at the same time allowing them to develop the skills necessary to become successful in the future.”

“As the boundaries between robots and humans become increasingly blurred, schools can no longer be an institution that produces students who fail to grasp the realities of the world and fail to apply the knowledge they have acquired throughout the years.”--by Mr. Soo Seng Poh

Co-curricular Activities


Chung Ling High School is best known for its commitment and excellence in co curricular activities and thus had won many prizes and trophies while achieving great heights whether district level, state level, national level or even international level competitions

Academics


Our teachers are dedicated in teaching students the 21 century teaching method which involves reading, writing, listening and the most importantly, speaking. That is why Chung Ling High School produces lots of students that are all rounders.

Facilities


Our school has many comfortable facilities for events and student use. One of them is the auditorium which is used paticularly during events in which involve educational talks. Outdoor facilities such as the school field and basketball courts are also provided to encourage students to play sport.
